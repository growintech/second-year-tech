### 1️⃣ Step 1 – Checkpoint 1: Present your progress

For today’s first activity, **you will have 10 minutes** to  prepare a 5 minute presentation about:

1. The **role** of each member, and the reason why each member chose that role;  
2. The **values** of the startup communicated through a social media post/interview. This must be **coherent** with the **CEO character** you chose. You should think about how your CEO would pitch your product;  
3. Your **problem statement**. 💡‼️As the name suggests, the problem statement must be a clear sentence that concisely explains the issue. *An example could be “restaurants waste lots of food due to poor resource management”. Not “we built an app that solves food waste”;*  
4. Your **problem tree**, explaining the thought process behind the 2/3 solutions you found (send your problem tree PDF to your instructors beforehand);  
5. The **feedback** you gathered with your feedback form, and what you learned about how you should shape your product idea.

1. The **Problem**: your problem statement and target audience;  
2. The **Solution**: your product;  
3. The **Market Size**: use [this prompt example](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SaYaIgQMiizNfCSuKkDxed3XPUquUWxQQWkTpRgeXZc/edit?usp=sharing) for Perplexity Deep Research to include TAM, SAM, and SOM with a [graph like this one](https://advertising.amazon.com/it-it/library/guides/tam-sam-som#1a);
