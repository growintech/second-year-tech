# **Mid Year Project – Phase 3**

Today marks the beginning of the final sprint\! It’s time to take your vision from a concept to a working reality and prepare to face our panel of investors.

### 1️⃣ Step 1 – Build your Startup Website

Turn your mockup into a functional digital product. This is your startup's home, and it needs to be both professional and interactive.

* **The Tech –** Use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build a single-page (or multi-page if you feel like it) website. Include a global Header and Footer\!\!  
* **The Mission** – Your website must clearly communicate your startup’s core values and mission. You can look at examples [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1hgVrsJcedr0YYf8Uj0D5F3D66FXvJRKHmBa6huLaQ4U/edit?usp=drive_link).  
* **The Must-Have** – Your website should include at least one highly interactive JavaScript section, like a map, a dynamic form, a search bar, an **iframe** etc. You can choose whatever looks perfect on your website, as long as it makes sense for your product. You are allowed to include some elements even if they do not work 100% (for example if you add a search bar, we don’t expect you to make it work flawlessly) but a fully functioning section is mandatory. You cannot only have partially functioning elements in your website. 

### 2️⃣ Prepare the Final Pitch

Present your startup to a Jury of Investors on **Feb 4th (Somme)** or **Feb 6th (Monti)**. The best team wins a **€15 gift card** (for each member\!) to invest in your passions\!  
To keep your presentation professional and logical, follow this specific flow (ideal for a team of 4):

1. **The Pitch Deck (Part 1):** start with a strong Hook, clearly define the Problem and your Solution  
2. **The Pitch Deck (Part 2):** show your Market Analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM) and present your Team

   *—\> this part should take no longer than 1 minute*

3. **The Mockup:** show your mockup design (in a PDF file ) to share your "ideal" vision  
   	*—\> this part should take no longer than 1 minute*  
4. **The Website:** switch to your functional site and **demonstrate the dynamic JS form** to the jury. Wrap up your speech with a **call to action**.

💡Prepare in advance: you will have little time to practice your speech in class. Find YOUR way to stand out and don’t forget the [delivery techniques](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/15yfYb_NzsoJEd4fyeid9TJfXwSb2sMdCq_b93tZdjTU/edit)\!
