Try pressing Ctrl+F inside the Elements panel and searching for backup or admin. Look for a div with style="display:none" — you can click it to expand it and see its content, or double-click the style attribute to remove display:none and make it visible.

display:none hides elements visually, but they remain fully in the HTML delivered to the browser. Anyone with DevTools can see them, copy their content, or toggle them visible with one click. This is a common mistake used to "hide" premium content, admin features, or sensitive data.

The lesson: Hidden elements are not secret elements. Never use CSS to hide sensitive information.
