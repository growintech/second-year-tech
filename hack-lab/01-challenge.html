HTML comments are visible to anyone who views the page source. Developers sometimes leave credentials, API keys, internal notes, or staging URLs in comments — and forget to remove them before going live. This has caused real data breaches at major companies.

The lesson: Never put secrets, credentials, or internal notes in HTML comments. Everything in the HTML is public.
