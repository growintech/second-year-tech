Sometimes text is hidden by making its colour match the background. The Elements tab in DevTools shows all HTML — look for a <p> or <span> with a suspicious inline style. You can also try selecting all text in the browser window with Ctrl+A / Cmd+A to reveal text that blends into the background.

FLAG ACCEPTED

Hiding text by matching its colour to the background gives zero security. The text exists in the DOM, can be selected, and shows up instantly in DevTools. This technique (called "CSS cloaking") is also used by scrapers to hide honeypot data in plain sight.

The lesson: If you don't want users to see data, don't send it to the browser at all.
